About Us


Welcome to NEEL Food products, your trusted destination for premium dairy products! We are committed to delivering the highest quality milk and dairy essentials, sourced from the finest farms and processed with the utmost care. Our products, ranging from fresh milk, curd, and paneer to delicious milkshakes and traditional sweets, are crafted to bring you the perfect balance of taste and nutrition. This is a public listed company having registered and corporate office at Vizag city of Andhra Pradesh state in India. The company was incorporated in the year 2023 and production commenced in 2025.


Mission

The company is engaged in the business of manufacturing and marketing of dairy products. The company is having its own dairy farm and processing plant. The company is having a wide range of dairy products like milk, curd, ghee, butter, paneer, ice cream, kulfi, milkshake, lassi, etc.
